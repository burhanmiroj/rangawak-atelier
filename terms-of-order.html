The entire rangawak range is available direct from our workshop and we deliver worldwide. please see our ordering details below.

Ordering Catalouge Pieces

An order can be made by phone or email

Examples of catalogue pieces can be viewed at the showroom or you can make apointment in West Sumatra and around.

An invoice will be sent to you and a 50% deposit is required before work can commence. There is usually a lead in time of approximately eight to ten weeks from order to delivery.

Each price can be tailored to your particular requirements in terms of size, finish of materials used.

Delivery

A delivery fee will be calculated according to the number and/or size of pieces and their destination. Advice of the delivery fee will be provided when an order is made.

There is an increased cost for orders with multiple items, please contact us for a delivery quote.

Pickup from the Rangawak workshop can be arranged.

More information

If you'd like more information on our products and ordering, or you would like a quote, please visit our contact page or call us directly to discuss any particular requirements you may have.
