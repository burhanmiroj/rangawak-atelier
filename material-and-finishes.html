Materials and finishes

Rangawak uses Surian Red Cedar as its main material and Mahogany as secondary material.

For Surian Red Cedar wood it has its own uniqueness. This wood has a reddish brown heartwood with pale red sapwood. The texture is moderately coarse. Surian has a definite cedar odor.

Moreover, Mahogany is one of the finest hardwoods. It can be highly figured out, beautiful with its consistent red color and also have a good benchmark for stability. Mahogany wood is usually used for fine furniture, cabinetry, wood carving, flooring and many other uses.
