About

RANGAWAK IS A WORKSHOP THAT CREATES HANDCRAFTED LIVING PRODUCTS WITH WOOD AS THE PRIMARY MATERIAL, BASED IN BARULAK, TANAH DATAR.

Rangawak was founded by Widi, as well as the only founder. Widi began Rangawak five years ago by learning and trying new things. He is the only woodworker in West Sumatera who adopts Japanese techniques.

Behind the name

THE MINANG PEOPLE ARE WELL-KNOWN FOR THEIR WANDERLUST, WHICH THEY PURSUE BOTH DOMESTICALLY AND OVERSEAS. WITH THE DESIRE TO CHANGE THEIR FATE, SEEK THEIR FORTUNE IN OTHER NATIONS, BELIEVE THAT THERE IS NO DEVELOPMENT IN MINANG LAND, AND A VARIETY OF OTHER REASONS, THEY LEAVE THEIR HOMETOWN.

The Minang language is used not only in the Rangawak brand name, but also in the titles of the wood workshop's items.
